Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information WPilot AI collects when you purchase or use our WordPress plugin bundle, how we use it, and the choices and rights you have over your data.

WPilot AI ("WPilot AI", "we", "us") is a bundle of AI-powered WordPress and WooCommerce plugins operated by PixelNX. We respect your privacy and collect only what we need to sell, deliver, and support the product. This policy applies to our sales pages, account area, and support channels.

1 Information We Collect

When you buy or use WPilot AI, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and billing details (processed by our payment partner).
  • Your transaction ID and purchase history for the license you own.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Any messages, attachments, or details you share when contacting support.

2 How We Use It

We use the information above to:

  • Process your purchase and issue your license and downloads.
  • Deliver plugin updates, security patches, and new versions.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Improve our products using aggregated, anonymized usage data.

3 AI Features & Your Server

WPilot AI plugins run on your own WordPress server. The content inside your site — products, orders, customer data, images, and uploads — stays on your server and is not sent to us.

Some features (for example description writing, chat, translation, or virtual try-on) send data to third-party AI providers such as Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI, Google Gemini, Replicate, or FAL AI — but only when you enable those features and connect your own API keys. That processing is governed by the provider's own terms and privacy policy.

4 Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with vetted service providers who help us operate — payment processing, email delivery, analytics, and support — and only under confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information where required by law.

5 Cookies

Our website uses essential, analytics, and affiliate-tracking cookies. You can learn more and manage your choices in our Cookie Policy.

6 Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as we provide the service to you. Purchase records are retained for up to seven years to meet tax and accounting obligations. You may request deletion of the rest at any time via our Data & Privacy Requests page.

7 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Submit a request through the Data & Privacy Requests page or by contacting support.

8 Security

We protect your data with SSL encryption in transit, PCI-compliant payment processing, restricted internal access, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission is 100% secure, but we work hard to safeguard your information.
