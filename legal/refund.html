1 30-Day Guarantee

Every WPilot AI purchase is covered by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason within 30 days of your purchase date, contact us and — provided your request meets the eligibility conditions below — we'll process a full refund to your original payment method.

2 Eligibility

To keep the guarantee fair and sustainable for everyone, a refund is available when:

  • Your request is made within 30 days of your original purchase date.
  • You have not consumed more than 50% of your AI usage allowance or otherwise over-used the platform's resources.
  • There is no evidence of misuse, abuse, or fraudulent activity on the account.
  • One refund per customer, per product.

3 How to Request

To request a refund, email support@pixalab.ai with the subject line "Refund Request – [Your Order ID]", or open a ticket at our support portal, and include:

  • The email address used for the purchase.
  • Your order / transaction ID.
  • Confirmation that you have deactivated and removed the plugins and any downloaded assets from your site(s).
  • Your reason for the refund (optional).

You don't need to explain why. Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment provider.

4 What's Not Covered

  • Requests made after the 30-day window has closed.
  • Accounts that have consumed more than 50% of their AI usage allowance or over-used platform resources.
  • Any account showing signs of misuse, abuse, or fraudulent activity.
  • Agency, Commercial, or Reseller licenses where you have already resold licenses or provisioned client sites.
  • Third-party costs you incurred (such as AI API usage, hosting, or domain fees).

5 Chargebacks

Please contact us first — we're happy to help and refunds are fast. Filing a fraudulent chargeback instead of requesting a refund may result in immediate revocation of your license and access. Questions? Email support@pixalab.ai.
