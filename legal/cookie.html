Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies the WPilot AI website uses, why we use them, and how you can control them.

We use a small number of cookies to keep the site working, understand how it's used, and credit the affiliates who refer customers to us. You're in control — see "Managing Cookies" below.

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your actions and preferences and provide a better, more consistent experience.

2 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for basic site operation — things like maintaining your session and keeping the checkout secure. Because the site can't function properly without them, they cannot be switched off.

3 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors find and use our pages so we can improve them. All of this data is aggregated and anonymized — it is not used to identify you personally.

4 Marketing Cookies

When you arrive through an affiliate link, marketing cookies (via LaunchPad) help us attribute your purchase to the correct affiliate partner. They may also support retargeting so you see relevant messaging about WPilot AI.

5 Managing Cookies

You can accept, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the site work. To opt out of Google Analytics specifically, you can install the official Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Questions? Email support@pixalab.ai.
